Spicy Pineapple Brown Sugar Chicken

Description (should this heading be smaller?)

This spicy brown sugar chicken will knock your socks off.

I've grilled these bad boys a number of times and have never been disappointed. Neither will you!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Pineapple Juice
  • 6 tablespoon BBQ Sauce
  • 6 tablespoon Brown Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Chili Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1.5 pound Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Whisk together pineapple juice, Brown sugar, BBQ sauce, chili powder, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Place chicken in a gallon ziplock bag. Pour remaining marinade over chicken. Seal bag and refrigerate 2 to 4 hours.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade. Grill until done, 12 to 15 minutes.
  4. Reserve 1/4 cup of marinade to brush on chicken before taking off the grill if you want spicer chicken.
