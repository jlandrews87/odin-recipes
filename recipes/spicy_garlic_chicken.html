Spicy Garlic Chicken

Description (should this heading be smaller?)

This spicy garlic and mustard chicken will knock your socks off.

I've grilled this bad boy a number of times and have never been disappointed. Neither will you!

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoon Yellow Mustard
  • 2 tablespoon Lime Juice
  • 2 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 4 teaspoon Minced Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Chili Garlic Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1 teaspoon Honey
  • 0.5 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1.5 pound Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Aquire chicken
  2. Aquire garlic
  3. Aquire spicy
  4. Mix those bad boys together
