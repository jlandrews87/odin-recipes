Spicy Garlic Chicken

Description (should this heading be smaller?)

This spicy garlic and mustard chicken will knock your socks off.

I've grilled this bad boy a number of times and have never been disappointed. Neither will you!

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoon Yellow Mustard
  • 2 tablespoon Lime Juice
  • 2 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 4 teaspoon Minced Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Chili Garlic Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha
  • 1 teaspoon Honey
  • 0.5 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1.5 pound Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except the chicken to a medium bowl and whisk to combine.
  2. Transfer the marinade mixture to a large ziptop plastic bag, add the chicken, seal, and squish contents around to evenly coat the chicken.
  3. Place bag in the fridge to marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 24 hours.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium-high heat (or indoor grill pan) and grill the chicken for about 7 minutes, or until done; flip intermittently to ensure even cooking.
  5. Optionally garnish with fresh herbs and serve with reserved marinade, if desired.
  6. If desired, before transferring the marinade to a ziptop bag, you can reserve about 1/4 cup to serve as a dipping sauce for the finished chicken.
